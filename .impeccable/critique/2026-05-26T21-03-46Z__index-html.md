## Anti-Patterns Verdict

Not AI-generated. Signature hooks (squiggle, lowercase nav, kbd badges, tinted neutrals) are too specific to be reflex output. Slop test passes on chrome AND content. Second-order reflex check is borderline — the digital-garden / personal-feed shape is saturated, but the bio content (named projects, hobbies, prose voice) carries the site past template territory.

Detector binary missing from impeccable install; fallback in-page scan reported zero true-positive structural triggers.

## Priority Issues

### [P1] Email-as-copy has zero pre-click affordance
The primary inbound conversion is a silent clipboard write. Recruiters expect mailto: behavior; some will think the link is dead.
- **Fix:** microcopy ("email (copy)"), split into two affordances, or hover tooltip.
- **Suggested command:** `clarify`

### [P1] Home-tab composition has a ~300px vertical hole on desktop
Bio cell 629px, photo cell 343px. Empty rectangle right of paragraphs 3–5 reads as unfinished. Worse in dark mode.
- **Fix:** Shorten bio paragraph 3, sticky photo, or reduce photo column width.
- **Suggested command:** `layout` or `shape`

### [P2] Muted text fails WCAG AA contrast
`#7e7c94` on `#f5f4fa` is contrast 3.69 (fails AA 4.5). Cool descriptions, dates, domain badges, tab labels at rest. Dark mode: `#7a7693` vs `#1e1c22` is 3.89, also under.
- **Fix:** `#6a6884` on light (~4.7), `#8e8aab` on dark (~4.6). Single-token change.
- **Suggested command:** `harden`

### [P2] Cool entry titles drop the squiggle hover
`stylesheet.css:313` explicitly suppresses the signature on the most-hovered links. Quiet design erosion not justified in DESIGN.md.
- **Fix:** Restore the squiggle on cool titles, or carve the exception into DESIGN.md.
- **Suggested command:** `polish`

### [P3] Centered `<h1>` over left-aligned body is an alignment seam
`.name.centered` centers the h1; bio is left-aligned; contact line is centered. The center/left/left/left/left/center stack reads as indecision.
- **Fix:** Left-align the name. Contact line can stay centered or also move left.
- **Suggested command:** `typeset`
